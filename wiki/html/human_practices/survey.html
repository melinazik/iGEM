Part 1: General understanding of biological concepts

The goal of first section of the survey was to understand the general understanding of biological concepts from the public. It consisted of 11 questions, all of them having a correct answer. During the process of the question answering, we made it clear to the participants that we could not help them or answer them any questions as we would bias the results. After the completion of the questionnaire, we discussed with the participants and analyzed the questions and the correct answers.

Here are the questions of the first part of the survey. The color that shows when the question box is hovered represents if the statement is right or wrong. Green is for right and red means wrong. Once the questions are clicked, statistics on the specific question answers are shown.

After the completion of the survey, once the answers were analyzed, we created a slideshow with the correct answers of each statement. We shared this slideshow with the public during our event Lab on the Street on the 9th of October, in Alexandroupolis, Greece along with the Blood Donation event that we organized at the same day.
