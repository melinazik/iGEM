Plant Synthetic Biology

Best Plant Synthetic Biology Special Prize

This award is designed to celebrate exemplary work done in plant synthetic biology. Did you build a project in a plant chassis? Did you submit plant parts to the Registry? This award could also be given to a team working with algae or another photosynthetic chassis.

To compete for the Best Plant Synthetic Biology prize, please describe your work on this page and also fill out the description on the judging form.
